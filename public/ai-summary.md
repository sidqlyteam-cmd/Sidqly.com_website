# Sidqly AI Summary

Sidqly is a premium Islamic operating platform for verified giving, protected dignity, and clear impact.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Verified Giving**: Manual payment review and multi-stage proof approval workflows.
- **Protected Dignity**: Shariah-aligned recipient privacy and dignity-safe data management.
- **Clear Impact**: Board-ready reporting and automated donor updates with verified evidence.

## Who Sidqly Is For

- **Mosques**: Friday collections, Zakat committees, and community welfare.
- **Islamic Charities**: High-impact operations, intake management, and proof of impact.
- **Qurbani Providers**: Animal tracking, slaughter proof, and delivery logistics.
- **Ramadan Drives**: Ration pack distribution and Iftar/Suhoor meal batch management.
- **Corporate CSR**: Professional reporting for corporate Zakat and impact programs.
- **Vendors & Volunteers**: Dedicated portals for fulfillment and field coordination.
