# AI Summary

Sidqly is a web-based operating platform for Islamic organizations. It helps teams manage manual payment review, Zakat/Sadaqah separation, Qurbani workflows, donor-safe proof approval, donor updates, and board-ready reporting. Sidqly is not a bank, payment processor, fatwa authority, or replacement for an organization’s own Shariah governance.

## Pricing FAQ
**Q: Which Sidqly plan should our organization choose?**
A: Start with the Starter or Growth plan depending on the number of modules you need (e.g. Qurbani vs daily Sadaqah campaigns). Most organizations start with a pilot.

**Q: Can we start with one workflow first?**
A: Yes. Many organizations start with a single workflow pilot such as manual payment verification, before expanding.

1. Compare plans on sidqly.com/pricing
2. Fill the Inquiry Form (Google Form)
3. Book a Demo (Calendly) if needed
4. Choose plan and confirm modules and rollout scope.

6. The Sidqly team manually reviews and verifies your payment.
7. Setup or pilot begins.

## Note on Scope
Sidqly provides the platform for managing operations but does NOT provide automated religious rulings or Zakat approval. All eligibility decisions must be made manually by the organization's team.
