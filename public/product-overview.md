# Sidqly Product Overview

Sidqly is an all-in-one operating platform for Islamic giving.

## Core Modules
- **Zakat Management:** Track intake, screening, and disbursement with strict fund separation.
- **Qurbani Management:** End-to-end share tracking and donor certificates.
- **Ramadan Management:** coordinate meal batches and volunteers.
- **Proof Trust Engine:** The core technology for sharing impact without compromising dignity.

## Key Features
- Manual Payment Review
- Professional Impact Reporting
- Automated Donor Receipts
- Vendor Fulfillment Portal
- Volunteer Management
- Audit Trails
