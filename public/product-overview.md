# Sidqly Product Overview

## What is Sidqly?

Sidqly is not just a donation form; it is a premium operating platform designed for the complex needs of modern Islamic organizations. It bridges the gap between receiving a donation and proving its impact while maintaining the highest standards of Shariah compliance and recipient dignity.

## Problem Sidqly Solves

- **Manual Chaos**: Replaces messy WhatsApp groups and scattered Excel sheets with a unified system.
- **Trust Deficit**: Provides verified proof (photos/videos) that has been manually reviewed and approved.
- **Operational Complexity**: Manages animal shares for Qurbani, ration bags for Ramadan, and Zakat eligibility logs.
- **Reporting Burden**: Automates board-ready reports and donor impact certificates.

## Trust & Dignity

Sidqly's "Proof Trust Engine" ensures that field evidence is reviewed by management before becoming donor-visible. Sensitive recipient information is strictly protected, ensuring aid is delivered with dignity.

## Implementation & Pilots

Organizations are encouraged to start with a "Pilot Launch" (e.g., a single Ramadan drive or Qurbani campaign) to see the operational clarity Sidqly brings before scaling to all services.
