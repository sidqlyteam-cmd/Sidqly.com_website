# Sidqly Product Overview

Sidqly is a modular SaaS platform designed for high-impact Islamic giving.

## Core Modules
- Zakat & Sadaqah: Fund separation and distribution tracking.
- Qurbani: Animal tracking, slaughter proof, and donor updates.
- Ramadan: Ration management and Iftar drive coordination.
- Proof & Trust: Manual review and approval of field activities.
- Vendor & Volunteer: Role-based access and fulfillment tracking.
- Reporting: Board-ready certificates and audit-ready data.
