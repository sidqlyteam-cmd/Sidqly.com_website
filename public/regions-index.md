# Sidqly Regions Index (AI Readable)

Sidqly supports organizations globally. The core operational system adapts to various regional needs for managing Islamic giving.

## Key Regions
- United Kingdom
- Europe
- North America
- Canada
- Gulf / MENA
- South Asia

## Global Standard
Sidqly enforces operational tracking, proof review, reporting, and donor communication universally. However, religious, legal, tax, and financial decisions remain with the organization's authorized reviewers in their respective regions. Public pages are provided in simple English to facilitate browser translation and AI summarization.
